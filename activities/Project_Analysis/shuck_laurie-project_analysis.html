Project Analysis for Creative Designs & Beyond

Created by: Laurie Shuck

Date: 11-01-2013

Site Intent

The goals for the Creative Designs & Beyond website is to generate new residential and commercial customers and showcase the company’s prior work. The owners, Mike and Sandi Nagy, would like the site to convey the image of a trusted family business that has their client's best interests in mind. The owner's would also like the site to be visually appealing and user friendly.

The goals for the user's of the Creative Designs & Beyond website include finding information about residential/commercial exterior remodeling, viewing images of the company’s prior work, scheduling a free in-home estimate, and/or locating the company’s contact information.

Currently, the Creative Designs & Beyond website does not have a form to schedule a free in-home estimate. In addition, the site is in need of a redesign to help with visual appeal, and to make the site more user friendly. The header navigation with the text “Click for more info on,” needs to be streamlined. The site has plenty of great images and content; they just need to be rearranged to create a more visually appealing and user friendly site.

Competitive Analysis

There are many websites that offer exterior remodeling; however there are not many that have enough data to rank their sites, according to the analytics website Alexa.com. The Creative Designs & Beyond website falls into the category of not having enough data to rank its site. The competitors that I have located each have a homepage that features a large image and have a form to schedule an estimate. They sites are visually appealing and user friendly; however each has some design issues that should be addressed.

Targeted Audience

The targeted audience for exterior home remodelers are college educated, live in a household with a starting income of $75,000 a year and are typically working professionals. In addition, they own their homes and take great pride in its appearance. The ages of the targeted audience range from 35-65 and they typically have a family.

Jennifer works as the CEO at a small startup tech company. She is happily married and has two young children in elementary school. The time Jennifer spends with her family is precious, and she would like to create a backyard oasis that will be enjoyed by her family for many years to come.

Jennifer is very tech savvy. She has a firm grasp on technology and uses it frequently to conduct business, shop and interact with family and friends. She owns an iPhone, an iPad, as well as two laptop computers.

Since Jennifer spends a great deal of time working, she does not have time to shop around for the best deal. Instead, she would rather purchase quality, high-end products made with the best materials.

When Jennifer visits the lettherebeshade.com website, she is looking for images that show the quality of the company's prior work, as well as the contact information for the company. She would be willing to fill out a form to schedule a free in-home estimate if she could schedule an exact date and time.

Steve Jackson

Location: Palo Alto, CA

Gender: Male

Age: 42

Occupation: Director of Technical Support at a large IT company

Steve works as a Director of Technical Support at a large IT company. He is constantly in meetings and does not have time for small talk. Steve has been married for 23 years and has three children. Steve and his wife have recently become grandparents, and would like to have a beautiful setting for the many family photos they will be taking in the future.

Steve is extremely tech savvy. He thinks, “If you are a company without an internet presence that you must not want to remain in business.” When he goes to a website, he wants it to make sense, or he gets frustrated. If the website is not up to his standards, he will move on until he finds one that does meet his standards. Steve believes that if a website is not designed well that the company is not run well. Steve bases his purchasing decisions on the look and feel of a website, as well as, the quality of the work the company has done in the past. Steve owns a smartphone, a tablet and a laptop.

When Steve visits the lettherebeshade.com website, he is looking for images of sunrooms and adjustable louvered patios. He would like to fill out a form to schedule a free in-home estimate because he does not have time to call and he is in a hurry.

Susan Davis

Gender: Female

Age: 48

Susan is a married and is a part-time librarian. Her son is moving back to the Bay Area with his wife and their three beautiful daughters. Susan does a majority of her web searches while she is at work, but is slowly learning how to access the Internet on her iPhone.

When Susan visits the lettherebeshade.com website, she is looking for images of adjustable louvered patios that she can print off to show to her husband. She jots down the company’s phone number on the printed images, so she can call to receive a free in-home estimate if her husband likes the images.
