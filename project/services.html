Services Offered

Making your patio remodeling dreams a reality may be easier than you imagined.

Solid patio covers provide protection from sun and rain. Our insulated patio covers feature a laminated panel that is resistant to changes in temperature.

Lattice Patio Covers allow you to select the amount of shade or sunlight you desire by adjusting the size and spacing of the lattice tubes. Lattice Patio Covers provide a comfortable environment while moderating the seasons temperatures. In addition, solid patio covers allow for easy installation of ceiling fans and other lighting fixtures on structurally reinforced panels.

By installing a patio cover you can transform your patio into an outdoor room attached to your house. The type of cover you purchase will determine whether it will be a room that is part of your backyard or a room that is part of your home where you can lounge around watching television.
